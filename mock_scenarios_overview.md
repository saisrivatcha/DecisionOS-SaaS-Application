# Mock Scenarios Overview

Here is a full breakdown of the 10 unique, pre-built mock scenarios available in the "✨" autofill menu for your presentation. Each scenario comes with specific customer context and 3 unique AI-suggested strategies.

---

## 1. Pricing Objection (CFO Pushback)
**Customer:** Acme Corporation
**Context:** The CFO is pushing back on our renewal pricing and wants a 20% discount. They are actively evaluating a cheaper competitor and their evaluation window closes in 12 days.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Executive Business Review:** Schedule an EBR with VP-level stakeholders to realign on value, address concerns, and confirm next steps.
2. **Renewal Incentive:** Offer a multi-year commitment with a modest discount and enhanced SLA guarantee.
3. **Hold Position:** Maintain pricing, send ROI evidence report, and allow the evaluation to finish.

---

## 2. Churn Risk (Low Adoption)
**Customer:** GlobalRetail Ltd.
**Context:** Usage has dropped by 40% in the last quarter. Our main champion left the company, and the new VP of Ops hasn't logged into the platform in 45 days.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) On-site Re-onboarding:** Offer a free, half-day on-site training workshop for the new VP's team.
2. **Downgrade Offer:** Proactively offer a lower tier to keep them as a customer while they transition.
3. **Do Nothing:** Send standard renewal notice at 30 days out.

---

## 3. Upsell Opportunity (Cross-department)
**Customer:** Meridian Health
**Context:** The product team is fully deployed. Now Legal and Finance are asking for read-only access to our analytics module, but they balked at the $400/seat price.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Site License Upgrade:** Pitch a flat-rate 'Unlimited Read-Only' site license for the entire org.
2. **Bundle Discount:** Offer a 40% discount on read-only seats if they buy 50+ seats upfront.
3. **Free Trial:** Give Finance 30 days of free access to prove value before charging.

---

## 4. Compliance Block (Security Audit)
**Customer:** FinBank Global
**Context:** Their InfoSec team just flagged our recent sub-processor change. They are threatening to halt deployment until we pass a custom 200-question security audit.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Executive Security Briefing:** Our CISO gets on a call with their CISO to review our updated SOC2 report and bypass the questionnaire.
2. **Outsource Audit Response:** Use our compliance partner (Vanta/Drata) to auto-fill the 200 questions.
3. **Push Back:** Refuse the custom audit based on clause 4.2 in the MSA.

---

## 5. Feature Request Escalation
**Customer:** StartupXYZ
**Context:** They are demanding a custom integration with their bespoke internal tool. The CEO emailed our CEO directly saying they will churn if it's not built this quarter.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) API Solutions Architect Session:** Offer a free consultation with our Solutions Engineer to show them how to build it themselves using our public API.
2. **Paid Professional Services:** Offer to build it for them for a one-time $25k Professional Services fee.
3. **Commit to Q4 Roadmap:** Agree to build it, but push the delivery timeline to Q4.

---

## 6. Implementation Delay (Resource Bottleneck)
**Customer:** HealthPlus
**Context:** Implementation is 3 weeks behind because their IT team hasn't provided the necessary database access.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Executive Escalation:** Escalate to the executive sponsor to unblock IT.
2. **Technical Workaround:** Use mock data temporarily to proceed with training while IT provisions access.
3. **Pause Implementation:** Formally pause billing and implementation until IT is ready.

---

## 7. Payment Dispute (Missing Invoice)
**Customer:** Logistics Pro
**Context:** Customer claims they never received the invoice and are refusing to pay late fees.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Waive Late Fees:** Waive the late fee as a one-time courtesy and set up auto-pay.
2. **Split the Difference:** Offer a 50% reduction in late fees.
3. **Enforce Contract:** Hold firm on standard policy due to 90-day delinquency.

---

## 8. Competitor Threat (Discounting)
**Customer:** EduTech Solutions
**Context:** Competitor offered them a 50% discount to switch.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Value-based Counter:** Highlight switching costs and missing competitor features.
2. **Price Match:** Offer a temporary 1-year discount to match competitor pricing.
3. **Product Bundle:** Throw in premium features for free instead of dropping price.

---

## 9. Executive Sponsor Left
**Customer:** CloudNet
**Context:** Our main champion and buyer just left for a competitor.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Map New Org Chart:** Immediately engage the interim leader with a summary of our past ROI.
2. **Grassroots Campaign:** Empower daily end-users to advocate for the tool to the new leadership.
3. **Executive Outreach:** Have our CEO reach out directly to their C-Suite to reaffirm partnership.

---

## 10. Major Bug (Data Loss)
**Customer:** FinServe Corp
**Context:** A bug in our recent release caused them to lose 4 hours of reporting data.
**AI Strategies:**
1. **(Recommended) Root Cause Analysis (RCA) + Credit:** Provide a full RCA document within 24 hours and a 1-month SLA credit.
2. **Premium Support Upgrade:** Offer 6 months of free Platinum Support as an apology.
3. **Do Nothing:** Wait for them to initiate a formal complaint or lawsuit.
