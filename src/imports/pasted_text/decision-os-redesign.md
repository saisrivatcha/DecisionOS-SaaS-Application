# Product Vision

DecisionOS

### "Every important business decision becomes a reusable organizational asset."

Companies lose millions because decisions disappear inside meetings, Slack, emails, and employees' memories.

DecisionOS captures every important decision, explains why it was made, measures whether it worked, and helps future teams make better decisions.

The platform is not an AI assistant.

It is the company's Decision Memory.

---

# Design Philosophy

The interface should answer one question per page.

Never overwhelm the user.

Users should always know:

* What requires my attention?
* Why is this important?
* What information do I need?
* What are my options?
* What happened previously?
* What happened after similar decisions?

Everything else should disappear.
